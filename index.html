Who I Am

I'm Asongna Brilan Nso Nkengafac, an Electronics engineer and embedded systems developer passionate about building technology that makes a measurable difference — from building environmental conservation hardwares and physical AI-powered Robots to community-run makerspaces.

I co-founded PlastiBytes to tackle environmental plastic pollution through smart hardware; HWIVC to grow a thriving hardware innovation ecosystem; and LEVON Robotics to push the frontier of intelligent machines.

My work spans firmware engineering (ESP-IDF / FreeRTOS), machine learning on the edge, and community leadership. I believe great technology is inseparable from the communities that use it.
